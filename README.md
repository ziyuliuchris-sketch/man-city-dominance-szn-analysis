Quantifying Dominance: A Data Science Analysis of Manchester City's 2022-23 Season

Project Overview:
Manchester City's 2022-23 season is widely regarded as one of the greatest seasons in modern soccer history. Although many people attribute their success to star players or Pep Guardiola's tactics, I wanted to investigate whether their dominance could be objectively measured through data. This project uses Python and statistical analysis to compare Manchester City with other elite European clubs from the 2022-23 season. Rather than focusing only on goals scored, this project examines how Manchester City balanced attacking production, defensive control, and overall game management.
